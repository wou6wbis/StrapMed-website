StrapMed.com

StrapMed is a tunisian startup. It helps the elderly who forget to take their medicine and make it a lot easier for the ones surrounding them, by providing a strap to remind them of the medication time and an easily portable pillbox. Our secret sauce is the connection via Bluetooth between the two components.
